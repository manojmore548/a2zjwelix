Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2023

At A2Z Jwelix, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use the A2Z Jwelix mobile application (the “Service”).

Information Collection and Use

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. This may include both personal and usage data.

Personal Data

When using our Service, you may be asked to provide personal details that can be used to identify or contact you. This information may include:

  • Email address
  • First name and last name
  • Phone number
  • Business details (name, address, etc.)
  • Cookies and usage data

Usage Data

We may also collect data on how the Service is accessed and used. This may include information such as your device's IP address, browser type, pages visited, and other diagnostic data.

Use of Data

A2Z Jwelix uses the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain our Service
  • To notify you about changes to our Service
  • To enable interactive features
  • To provide customer support
  • To gather feedback and improve the Service
  • To monitor usage patterns and system performance
  • To detect and address technical issues

Transfer of Data

Your data may be transferred to and stored in systems located outside your jurisdiction. A2Z Jwelix ensures that adequate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Disclosure of Data

We may disclose personal data in the following cases:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect the rights and property of A2Z Jwelix
  • To prevent or investigate potential wrongdoing
  • To ensure the safety of our users or the public
  • To defend against legal claims

Data Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. We implement appropriate measures to minimize risk.

Third-Party Service Providers

We may employ third-party companies to facilitate the Service, provide services on our behalf, or help us analyze usage. These third parties have access to your data only to perform these tasks and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Links to External Sites

Our Service may contain links to other websites not operated by A2Z Jwelix. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these sites and recommend reviewing their privacy policies individually.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

A2Z Jwelix reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the “Last Updated” date will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
